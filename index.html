About Us

NOOR HARDWARE & DOOR SHOP

NOOR Hardware, located in Mirganj, Gopalganj, Bihar, started with a small investment and has grown on the foundations of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Today, we are proud to be a leading wholesaler in the industry, providing products that our customers can rely on. Currently, we sell our products in several districts in Bihar. However, our future vision is expansive. We plan to open our own door factory and company to produce 99% top-quality products, setting new standards in the industry. We aim to serve all areas of Bihar and eventually extend our reach to every corner of India.
